WELCOME TO MY PAGE. HERE YOU WILL DISCOVER WHY CATS ARE WONDERFUL.

I am so glad you have decided to click on my page. Here I am going to dive into the wonderful world of the felines! As I have said I am just going to write a little on why all felines are so wonderful. Even more so cats! Cats are more so wonderful than, oh lets say a tiger because you do not have to worry about being dinner! So let us delve deeper. Though cats are dearly loved these days, sadly it has not always been that way. There was a period of fear during many witchcraft trials where black cats were deemed evil and people would hunt them and kill them. But luckily most people have come around to see that not only are cats, more specifically black cats not evil but they are loving and kind and can even heal a broken heart! Not only that they are very entertaining! Between watching them have the zoomies to all their random facial expressions they will have you laughing or smile at the least!

    Below are three fun facts about cats!
  • Cats can produce over 100 distinct sounds.
  • Cats can purr at a frequency range of 25 all the way up to 150.
  • A cats purr has been linked to healing various ailments.
